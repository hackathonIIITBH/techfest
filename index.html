About Us

BIT BY BIT is an open ended , non stop "toolbox development " competition initiated by Technical board of IIIT Bhagalpur. This is an inter college competition which got participation from enthusiasts in different feilds . In this our aim is to inspire minds to unique problem specific toolboxes for proposed ideas or even open-ended real life problems.
